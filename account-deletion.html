Account Deletion

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of a Tintforge account and associated cloud gameplay data.

How to request deletion

  1. Email nawmlabs@gmail.com with the subject "Tintforge Account Deletion Request".
  2. Send the request from the email address connected to your Google Play Games or Google account if possible.
  3. Include your Tintforge player name or Google Play Games display name if available.

What we delete

After verifying the request, we delete or anonymize cloud data associated with the account where reasonably possible, including Firebase Authentication account records and user-specific gameplay data such as progress, economy state, reward claims, stage sessions, and related reward records.

What may be retained

Some purchase, refund, fraud-prevention, tax, accounting, security, or legal records may be retained as required or permitted by law. Local data stored on your device is not deleted by a cloud deletion request; you can remove it by clearing app data or uninstalling Tintforge.

Processing time

We aim to respond to deletion requests within a reasonable period after verification. If we need more information to verify the account, we will contact you by email.

For more information, see the Tintforge Privacy Policy.
