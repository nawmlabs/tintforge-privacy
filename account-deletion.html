Account Deletion

Effective date: April 29, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

This page explains how to request deletion of a Tintforge account and associated cloud gameplay data.

2. How to Request Deletion

  1. Email nawmlabs@gmail.com with the subject line: "Tintforge Account Deletion Request"
  2. Send the request from the email address connected to your Google Play Games or Google account if possible.
  3. Include your Tintforge player name or Google Play Games display name if available.

3. Data Deleted

After verifying the request, we delete or anonymize cloud data associated with the account where reasonably possible.

Data TypeDeletion Status
Firebase Authentication account recordsDeleted where reasonably possible
Gameplay progress and stage recordsDeleted
Economy state, rewards, and inventory recordsDeleted
Reward claims and stage session recordsDeleted or anonymized where reasonably possible

4. Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained as required or permitted by law, or where needed for security, fraud prevention, purchase fulfillment, accounting, tax, or legal compliance.

Data TypeReason
Purchase and refund recordsFraud prevention, accounting, support, and legal compliance
RevenueCat and Google Play Billing recordsManaged by those service providers under their own policies
Security and anti-abuse recordsTo protect the App and prevent duplicate rewards or unauthorized economy changes
Local device dataNot removed by cloud deletion; clear app data or uninstall Tintforge to remove local data

5. Processing Time

We aim to respond to deletion requests within a reasonable period after verification. If we need more information to verify the account, we will contact you by email.

6. Contact

For account deletion or privacy questions, contact us:
