Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

Tintforge ("the App") is a color puzzle game published by Nawm Labs. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you use Tintforge, Firebase Authentication may assign a user ID. If you connect Google Play Games or Google sign-in, we may receive account identifiers such as display name or email address provided by those services.

2.2 Gameplay Data

We store gameplay information needed to operate the game, including completed stages, stars, scores, stage sessions, reward claims, coins, hearts, item inventory, and related timestamps.

2.3 Purchase Data

For in-app purchases, we process product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase status, refund status, entitlement delivery records, and purchase event timestamps. Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat.

2.4 Advertising Reward Data

When you watch rewarded ads, Google AdMob and our servers may process reward transaction identifiers, reward type, reward amount, user ID, nonce, and server-side verification status.

2.5 Local App Data

Tintforge stores some data locally on your device, such as progress, economy state, settings, cached stage data, and cached remote assets.

2.6 Technical Data

Our service providers may process device, app, network, diagnostic, advertising, and anti-abuse information needed to operate Firebase, Google Play services, AdMob, Google Play Billing, and RevenueCat.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and support Google Play Games or Google sign-in
  • To save and sync game progress, rewards, economy state, and purchases
  • To deliver in-app purchases and verify purchase or refund events
  • To show rewarded ads and validate rewards through server-side verification
  • To provide remote game assets and gameplay data
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, duplicate rewards, and unauthorized economy changes
  • To maintain, debug, secure, and improve the App
  • To respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests

6. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your Tintforge account and associated cloud gameplay data at any time.

  1. Email nawmlabs@gmail.com with the subject line: "Tintforge Account Deletion Request"
  2. Send the request from the email address connected to your Google Play Games or Google account if possible.
  3. Include your Tintforge player name or Google Play Games display name if available.

For details, visit the Tintforge Account Deletion page.

7. Children's Privacy

Tintforge is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can choose whether to connect Google Play Games or Google sign-in.
  • You can manage ad personalization through Google and device settings where available.
  • You can clear local app data through your device settings.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of cloud account data by contacting us.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including encrypted connections, Firebase access controls, authenticated server operations, and limited access to user-specific cloud records. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. International Processing

Information may be processed in countries other than where you live, including where our service providers operate systems and infrastructure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
