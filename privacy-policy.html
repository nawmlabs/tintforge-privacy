Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nawm Labs ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use Tintforge, including the Android app distributed through Google Play.

Developer and contact

Developer: Nawm Labs
App: Tintforge
Privacy contact: nawmlabs@gmail.com

Information we collect

Depending on how you use Tintforge, we may collect or process the following information:

  • Account information, such as your Firebase user ID and, when you sign in with Google Play Games or Google, account identifiers such as display name or email address provided by those services.
  • Gameplay and progress information, such as completed stages, stars, scores, stage sessions, reward claims, coins, hearts, item inventory, and related timestamps.
  • Purchase information, such as product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase status, refund status, entitlement delivery records, and purchase event timestamps.
  • Advertising reward information, such as rewarded ad transaction identifiers, reward type, reward amount, user ID, nonce, and server-side verification status.
  • Local app data stored on your device, such as game progress, economy state, settings, cached stage data, and cached remote assets.
  • Technical information processed by service providers, such as device, app, network, diagnostic, advertising, and anti-abuse information needed to operate Firebase, Google Play services, AdMob, Google Play Billing, and RevenueCat.

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate users and support Google Play Games or Google sign-in.
  • Save and sync game progress, rewards, economy state, and purchases.
  • Deliver in-app purchases and verify purchase or refund events.
  • Show rewarded ads and validate rewards through server-side verification.
  • Provide remote game assets and gameplay data.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, duplicate rewards, and unauthorized economy changes.
  • Maintain, debug, secure, and improve the app.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests.

Third-party services

Tintforge uses third-party services that may process information under their own terms and privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase, including Authentication, Cloud Functions, Firestore, and Storage.
  • Google Play Games services for sign-in and gameplay account connection.
  • Google AdMob for rewarded ads and ad reward verification.
  • Google Play Billing for Android in-app purchases.
  • RevenueCat for purchase validation, entitlement processing, and purchase event handling.

Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We share information with service providers only as needed to operate Tintforge, including authentication, cloud storage, rewards, ads, purchases, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and support. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or to investigate abuse.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including encrypted connections, Firebase access controls, authenticated server operations, and limited access to user-specific cloud records. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Retention and deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or the app replaces cached data during normal operation. Cloud data is kept while needed to provide gameplay sync, rewards, purchase fulfillment, fraud prevention, support, and legal compliance.

You can request deletion of your Tintforge account and associated cloud gameplay data by visiting our Account Deletion page or by emailing nawmlabs@gmail.com. Some purchase, fraud-prevention, tax, accounting, security, or legal records may be retained as required or permitted by law.

Your choices

  • You can choose whether to connect Google Play Games or Google sign-in.
  • You can manage ad personalization through Google and device settings where available.
  • You can clear local app data through your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of cloud account data using the contact above.

Children

Tintforge is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

International processing

Information may be processed in countries other than where you live, including where our service providers operate systems and infrastructure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Tintforge after an update means the revised policy applies.
